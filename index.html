Survey Insights & How We Address Them

Key findings from the MB internal staff survey mapped to this solution

⏰

Issue: Time reconstruction is inaccurate

Attorneys reconstruct their day from memory, losing billable time and productivity for the day.

✅ Solution: Auto-detect captures activities in real-time from Outlook, Teams, and document editors so, no reconstruction of the day needed.

😤

Issue: Manual entry is tedious & time-consuming

Staff spend 20-40 minutes daily on time capturing, reducing productivity hours.

✅ Solution: One-click timer, quick-entry forms, and auto-capture reduce manual input to under 5 minutes/day.

💸

Issue: Ghost Practice costs R33,925/month

High licensing fees with limited automation or automation capabilities.

✅ Solution: Ghost Practice 2.0 is designed as a modern, cost-effective alternative with Automation architecture, reducing operational costs while improving capture rates and billing accuracy.

📊

Issue: Lack of visibility into team productivity

Managers struggle to assess utilization and workload distribution.

✅ Solution: Real-time Insights dashboard with category breakdowns, client allocation, and efficiency metrics for both individuals and teams.

🔗

Issue: Disconnected tools create data silos

Email, calendar, and billing live in separate systems with no integration.

✅ Solution: Similation integrations with Microsoft 365, Teams, and practice management systems via API. Single source of truth for all billable activities.
